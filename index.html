How to play

In the game, there are 100 people on a plane (including bots) jumping on an island to find items(guns, ammo, auxiliary and healing items) to shoot each other. When only one person survives, that person will get the top 1 and get to eat (chicken). There will be a circle (also known as the blue zone) forcing the player to move in the safe zone (called the playerzone). There is also a redzone arrow that will be bombed all over the area and the player must run out or into the house . If a player is shot dead , a crate full of that player's items will be left. The supply box will be dropped, inside there are high-class weapons and armor.
